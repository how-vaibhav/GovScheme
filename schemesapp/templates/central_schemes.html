Affordable housing scheme to provide houses to economically weaker sections and lower-income groups.

Prime Minister MUDRA Yojana

Micro-financing scheme to support small businesses and entrepreneurs with affordable loans.

Pradhan Mantri Scholarships

Educational scholarships for meritorious students to pursue higher education.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

Agricultural scheme promoting sustainable irrigation practices and crop productivity.

Ayushman Bharat

Health insurance scheme providing free medical treatment to eligible families.
