Our Vision

At GovAid, our vision is to empower the people of Sikkim with seamless access to government schemes across all sectors. We aim to bridge the gap between citizens and essential opportunities by providing timely, transparent, and comprehensive updates. Our mission is to make crucial information readily available, ensuring that no one misses out on the benefits designed to improve their lives.

Whether it's education, healthcare, agriculture, entrepreneurship, or social welfare, GovAid ensures that every resident stays informed, engaged, and ready to benefit from the resources meant for their growth and well-being.
