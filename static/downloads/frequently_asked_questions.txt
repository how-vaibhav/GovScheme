GovAid - Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Is registration required?
A: Yes, create an account to apply and track schemes.

Q2: How is eligibility calculated?
A: Based on your profile details and scheme criteria.

Q3: Can I apply for multiple schemes?
A: Yes, if you meet eligibility criteria.

Q4: How long does approval take?
A: It depends on the scheme and department workflow.

Q5: Where can I see updates?
A: Check Notifications and My Applications sections.
