How to Apply for Schemes - Quick Guide

Step 1: Complete your profile details.
Step 2: Open a scheme and click 'Check Eligibility'.
Step 3: If eligible, click 'Apply'.
Step 4: Enter required details and upload valid documents.
Step 5: Submit and track status from the applications page.

Best Practice:
- Double-check income and category before submission.
- Keep your phone number active for notifications.
